Author Instructions

01. Manuscript Preparation

General Requirements

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research papers that are not currently under review by another conference or journal.

The working language of the conference is English. All manuscripts must be written and presented in English.

Formatting & Style

Papers must be formatted according to the standard IEEE Conference Template.

  • Paper Size: A4.
  • Length: Maximum of 4 pages, including all figures, tables, and references.
  • File Format: Submissions must be in PDF format.
02. Submission Process

Blind Review Policy

InGARSS 2026 follows a peer-review process. Ensure that your initial submission does not contain author names or affiliations if the specific track requires double-blind review.

03. Final Submission & Copyright

Registration Requirement

At least one author of an accepted paper must register at the full (non-student) rate and present the paper at the symposium to be included in the proceedings.

Copyright Transfer

Accepted papers will require an electronic IEEE Copyright Form (eCF) before final publication in IEEE Xplore®.
