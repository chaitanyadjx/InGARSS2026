[ PURPOSE ]

2026 IEEE InGARSS

A premier event bringing together experts, researchers, engineers, and industry leaders to explore the intersection of Geoscience, Space, Satellite, remote sensing and Artificial Intelligence (AI).

The conference is a unique platform for showcasing cutting-edge research, fostering interdisciplinary collaborations, and discussing future directions in geospatial and remote sensing science.

The Mission

We aim to advance these fields and promote sustainable and ethical AI/ML practices in the geoscience domain by bringing together researchers, practitioners, and policymakers.
