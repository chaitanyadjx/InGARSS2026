01. PURPOSE

Advancing Earth Observation in the Global South

InGARSS 2026 bridges the gap between high-altitude data and ground-level impact. Hosted in India, this edition focuses on climate resilience, satellite startups, and deep learning.

Generative AI for synthetic satellite imagery and automated disaster damage assessment.

T-03

UAV & Robotics

Drone-based remote sensing for urban planning and high-resolution infrastructure mapping.
