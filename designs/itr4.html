PURPOSE

Advancing Geoscience

2026 IEEE India Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium (InGARSS 2026) is a premier event bringing together experts, researchers, and engineers to explore the intersection of Space, Satellite, Remote Sensing, and Artificial Intelligence.

The Mission

The conference is a unique platform for showcasing cutting-edge research and fostering interdisciplinary collaborations. We aim to promote ethical AI/ML practices in the geoscience domain by bridging the gap between theory and real-world application.
